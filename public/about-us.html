About Us

About Us

As high school students ourselves not too long ago, we experienced many frustrating nights working on homework with not help in reach. Now as college students, we aim to create a solution so high schoolers won’t continue to suffer the same fate. Ever since we decided it was important to help schools offer peer tutoring, we’ve learned, and will continue to learn, the many challenging yet fulfilling elements of entrepreneurship.

  • Apr 2019
    SEED SPOT’s 2-Day Launch Camp gave us a chance to draft our first business plan based on the real needs of high schools, and it solidified our identity as a social venture.
  • Aug 2019
    Joined ASU’s Venture Devils entrepreneurship program to clarify our business and unique services.
  • Dec 2019
    We were awarded substantial grant funding through Venture Devils’ Demo Day via the eSeed Challenge.
  • Jan 2020
    PeerSquared, Inc. becomes a registered public benefit corporation aiming to improve the education and future success of K-12 students.
  • Feb 2020
    Conduct research at high schools selected for our first pilot programs
  • Apr 2019
    SEED SPOT’s 2-Day Launch Camp gave us a chance to draft our first business plan based on the real needs of high schools, and it solidified our identity as a social venture.

We look forward to the results of our upcoming pilots in Arizona and hope many more schools will benefit from peer tutoring in the near future. Feel free to contact us using the message box at the bottom or just put in your contact info to stay tuned to our progress.

Michael Wang

Chief Executive Officer

Michael and his friends started their high school's first peer tutoring club 5 years ago to provide academic help and a safe environment to earn service hours and grow. Ke Liu

Chief Learning Officer

Ke Liu is an avid gardener, a hardcore runner, and a learning experience designer. Ke's experiences in different industries led her to an interest in studying human learning and a life goal to promote equity in education. She joined PeerSquared as the co-founder and the Chief Learning Officer.
